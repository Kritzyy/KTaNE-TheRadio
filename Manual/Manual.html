Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod The Radio

On the Subject of The Radio

The signal keeps breaking up...

SEE SECTION GL FOR A GUIDE LIST OF ALL RADIO CHANNELS.

  • On this module, an old radio can be found.
  • Above the radio is the radio’s barcode.
  • To solve this module, input the correct frequency and transmission, and turn the radio on at the correct time, given in table X
  • If an incorrect transmission or frequency is given, or submitted at the wrong time, a strike will be handed.
  • To reset the radio to Factory settings, press the top right button of the radio.

    Section B: Checking the correct frequency.

    To decide the desired frequency, go through the list of the correct country and find the first entry (in the brackets) which has at least one letter in the barcode.

    Section C: Checking the correct transmission.

    The transmission is "FM" if both rules apply:

    • If the first digit of the serial number is greater than the last.
    • If the first digit of the barcode is greater than the last.

    Else, the transmission is "AM"
